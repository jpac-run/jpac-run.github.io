About

Hello!

I'm João Pedro Chitas, a portuguese web-developer based in Lisbon.

Currently, I'm working at Runtime Revolution where I develop web-applications, with the use of the Ruby on Rails framework. Besides RoR, I've already worked with other frameworks and languages, such as React and C#. And, of course, the good old trio Html/CSS/Javascript.

During my time at Instituto Superior Técnico, where I took my BSc in Computer Engineering, I was a member of SINFO for 2 years, helping organizing this annual tech event, started by college students more than 20 years ago.

I'm open to freelance projects and sometimes I contribute to opensource projects, if I find there is an error or a feature is lacking that may come handy.

Being a Football lover, I've practised at a local club during my high school years, but only play with friends nowadays. Besides football, I enjoy to practice Padel in my spare time.
